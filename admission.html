Admission

chevron_right Submit an Application

The first step in our admission process is to submit an application. Our online application form is user-friendly and can be completed at your convenience. You'll be asked to provide basic information about your child, educational history, extracurricular interests, and any other relevant details. Link to Online Application Form


chevron_right Assessment (if applicable)

Depending on the grade level and program to which your child is applying, they may be required to undergo an assessment. This assessment helps us determine their academic readiness and ensures that they are placed in the appropriate grade level and program that best suits their needs and abilities. Our assessments are designed to be fair, objective, and reflective of our commitment to academic excellence.


chevron_right Parent and Student Interview

Once we have received your child's application, we will invite you and your child for an interview with members of our admissions team. This interview provides an opportunity for us to get to know your family better, understand your child's interests, strengths, and areas for growth, and discuss how Excel International Schools can support their educational journey. It's also a chance for you to ask any questions you may have about our school community, curriculum, extracurricular activities, or the admission process.


chevron_right Acceptance

After completing the admission process, including the assessment (if applicable) and the interview, our admissions committee will carefully review your child's application. We aim to make admissions decisions that are in the best interest of each individual student and the overall school community. Once a decision has been made, you will receive an acceptance letter welcoming your child to Excel International Schools!


chevron_right Enrollment

Upon acceptance, you will be provided with information about the enrollment process, including important dates, tuition and fees, and any additional documentation required. Our admissions team will be available to guide you through this process and answer any questions you may have.

We understand that choosing a school is a significant decision, and we are here to support you every step of the way. If you have any questions about our admission process or would like to schedule a campus tour, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to welcoming your family to the Excel International Schools community and embarking on this exciting educational journey together!
